1-up mushroom

Story

1-Up Mushrooms (sometimes parsed as 1 up Mushrooms,[1] Extra Life Mushrooms,[2] 1UP Mushrooms,[3] or 1-UP Mushrooms[4]) are green mushrooms that give the player an extra life. They are usually the rarest mushrooms, but they can still be found in almost every game a Super Mushroom has appeared in, and even some it has not. 1-Up Mushrooms are usually found in hidden blocks or in risky places for the player. They always grant the player a positive effect when collected. The above text is from the Super Mario Wiki and is available under a Creative Commons license.
